01 — About

Who I Am &
What Drives Me

I'm a final-year Computer Science & Engineering student at GITAM University, Visakhapatnam — and a lifelong Rockstar Games fan who has spent hundreds of hours not just playing GTA V and Red Dead Redemption 2, but studying them. The way open worlds are constructed, how NPCs behave, where physics systems break down — these are the things that fascinate me.

That obsession led me to start formally documenting bugs I find in Rockstar titles, treating every session as a QA exercise. I have already identified 4 reproducible bugs in GTA V and I'm actively expanding that research into RDR2.

On the development side, I've recreated a full environment from Uncharted: Drake's Fortune in Unreal Engine 5 and shipped a complete 2D platformer in Unity — giving me a developer's understanding of why bugs happen, not just that they exist. I believe the best game testers are the ones who genuinely love games and understand how they're made.

03 — Bug Reports

Real Bugs Found in
Rockstar Titles

I treat every GTA V and RDR2 session as a live QA exercise — hunting for reproducible defects and documenting them to professional standards: severity ratings, platform specifics, and step-by-step reproduction cases. Watch the proof videos embedded below.

BUG-GTA-001  ·  March 28, 2026

Heavily Damaged Front Tire Steering Failure Gets Overridden by Franklin's Special Ability

When the front tire is heavily damaged, the vehicle fails to turn left during normal driving. However, activating Franklin's special ability restores steering completely — as if no damage occurred. The problem returns immediately once the ability is deactivated.

Platform: GTA V · PC · v1.0.1013.33  ·  Location: Any open world location  ·  Frequency: 10/10 attempts

Major PC 10/10 Reproducible
⬇ Report PDF

BUG-GTA-002  ·  March 28, 2026

Invisible Wall in Parking Lot Blocks Cop Damage and Prevents Player from Shooting Outward

An invisible wall inside a parking lot completely blocks incoming police damage and prevents the player from firing outward. The player becomes trapped with cops surrounding the only exit, forcing them to absorb heavy damage with no viable escape route.

Platform: GTA V · PC · v1.0.1013.33  ·  Location: Parking lot (see footage)  ·  Frequency: 10/10 attempts

Major PC 10/10 Reproducible
⬇ Report PDF

BUG-GTA-003  ·  March 28, 2026

Bike Recovery Animation Skipped on Slopes — Angled Bike Position Causes Player to Teleport Onto Bike Instantly

After crashing a bike on a slope, the game fails to calculate the angled recovery approach. Instead of performing a proper lifting animation, the player instantly teleports onto the bike and it stands upright on its own — completely skipping the mounting sequence.

Platform: GTA V · PC · v1.0.1013.33  ·  Location: Any sloped surface  ·  Frequency: 5/10 attempts

Minor PC 5/10 Reproducible
⬇ Report PDF

BUG-GTA-004  ·  March 28, 2026

Attempting to Join Quick Heist Teleports Online Character Under the Map into a Skydiving State

The Quick Heist teleport system sends the character to an incorrect spawn point beneath the map geometry. The character spawns in a skydiving state with a parachute equipped — the entire map is visible above them. A hidden skydiving spawn point appears to be accidentally triggered by the heist matchmaking system.

Platform: GTA V · PC · v1.0.1013.33  ·  Location: GTA Online — any road  ·  Frequency: 2/5 attempts

Major PC · GTA Online 2/5 Reproducible
⬇ Report PDF
